Moderators:

Different users have access to different functions of the site. While anyone can do most things on the site, including reading and editing, administrators can access additional functions.

Moderator Abilities

These additional functions include:

  • Being able to quickly rollback undesirable edits.
  • Deleting and restoring pages, page histories, and uploaded files.
  • Locking (protecting) a page so it cannot be edited or renamed by users without admin rights or autoconfirmed status.
  • Blocking users from editing.
  • Editing the interface by changing system messages and skins.

Content

Having this status causes the CONTENT MODERATOR tag to appear on the user's userpage.
